Comprehensive Lubricant Solutions

ZICO PETROLEUM & MARKETING COMPANY LTD provides end-to-end Petroleum services across the entire value chain, from blending and production to refining, distribution, and retail. Our integrated approach ensures reliability, efficiency, and sustainability in all our operations.

Blending Plant

Our blending plant leverages advanced technology and modern infrastructure to formulate and produce high-quality lubricants and specialty petroleum products. With precision operations and strict quality control, we ensure reliability, efficiency, and environmental responsibility across every stage of production.

  • Formulation development and customization
  • Base oil and additive integration
  • Automated blending operations
  • Quality control and laboratory testing
  • Packaging and distribution management
