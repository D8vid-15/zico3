800 thousand, 1 million & 1.5 million Capacity Tank Construction

Edo state, Nigeria 2020-2025

A comprehensive storage infrastructure development initiative involving the construction of 1 million and 1.5 million-capacity petroleum product tanks. The project is designed to enhance bulk storage, distribution efficiency, and long-term supply security. Utilizing advanced engineering designs, high-grade steel, and cutting-edge fabrication technology, the tanks incorporate modern monitoring systems, corrosion protection, and automated safety controls to ensure operational reliability and environmental compliance.
