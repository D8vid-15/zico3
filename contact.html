Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

What products does ZPMCL offer?

ZICO Marketing and Petroleum Company Ltd offers a wide range of petroleum products like Lubricants for various industrial applications.

How can I become a distributor of ZPMCL products?

We have a distributor program for businesses interested in selling our products. Please contact our sales department at sales@zicooil.com or call +234 123 456 7890 to learn about requirements and opportunities.

What are your operating hours?

Our main offices are open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

How can I apply for employment at ZPMCL?

We welcome qualified candidates to apply through our website. You can also submit your resume to careers@zicooil.com. We review all applications and contact candidates whose skills match our current openings.
